INTRODUCING INTERZONE

InterZone building on Dash's groundbreaking Masternode technology, Interzone aims to add customizable attributes and expand blockchain-based distributed computing. The InterZone network is completly free and open-source, decentralized, secured by CPU/GPU proof of work, and puts private transactions front and center.

Community

Taking the concept of masternodes to the next level by laying a foundation for customization and a unique community centric sharing model. 25% per-block minter/node sharing through 250,000 blocks then 50% minter/node sharing after 500,000 blocks, forever!

Security

ITZ is build from the ground up with rock-soli 11-algorithm chained CPU/GPU minting. Easy one-click in client minting and GPU minting available utilizing third party mining software.
